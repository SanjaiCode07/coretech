About Sri Durgai Core Tech

Sri Durgai Core Tech & Sri Durgai Coated Sands is one of Coimbatore's trusted manufacturers of Shell Cores, Shell Moulds, and Resin Coated Sand.

With over 14 years of manufacturing excellence and a team backed by more than 20 years of technical experience, the company serves ferrous and non-ferrous casting industries with reliable and high-quality products.
